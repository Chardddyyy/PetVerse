Privacy Policy 🔒

Last updated: June 2025

What we collect

When you register on PetVerse, we collect your name, email address, and your pet's information (name and type). When you use our platform, we also collect your activity — posts, likes, comments, event attendance, and pets you follow.

How we use your information

We use your information to run your PetVerse account, show your activity in the community feed, and send you verification codes via email. We do not sell your data to anyone.

Passwords

Your password is never stored in plain text. We use bcrypt hashing, which means even our own team cannot read your password.

Community content

Posts, comments, and events you create are visible to all PetVerse community members. You may post anonymously using the anonymous option in the feed.

Donations

Donations go directly to Richard B (GCash: 09858337254) for stray animal welfare. PetVerse does not process or store payment information — all transactions are handled through GCash.

Your rights

You can update your profile information at any time through your account settings. To delete your account or request your data, contact us at 📞 09858337254.

Contact

For any privacy concerns, you can reach us by calling 09858337254.
